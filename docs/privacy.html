Privacy Policy — Calculator

Last updated: May 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Calculator mobile application ("the App", "we", "our") handles your information. By using the App you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

The App is designed to work fully offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information such as your name, email, phone number, or contacts.

All calculations, conversion history, and theme preferences are stored locally on your device only.

2. Crash and Performance Data

To improve app stability and performance, we use Google Firebase services (Crashlytics and Performance Monitoring). These services may automatically collect:

  • Anonymous crash stack traces
  • Device model, OS version, and app version
  • Anonymous performance metrics (cold start time, screen rendering)

This data is fully anonymous and is used solely to identify and fix bugs. No personal information is collected.

3. Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

  • INTERNET and ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — required by Firebase Crashlytics to upload anonymous crash reports.

The App does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, photos, or storage.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase (Crashlytics and Performance Monitoring) provided by Google LLC. Their privacy policy is available at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Retention

Local data (calculation history, settings) remains on your device until you uninstall the App or clear its data. Anonymous Firebase data is retained according to Google's Firebase data retention policies.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is rated for everyone (3+) and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
